Essential DVSA Knowledge

Show Me, Tell Me Questions

Before you start driving your practical test, the examiner will ask you two vehicle safety questions. Let's get you prepared.

Q: Tell me how you'd check that the brakes are working before starting a journey.

A: Brakes should not feel spongy or slack. Brakes should be tested as you set off. Vehicle should not pull to one side.

Q: Show me how you'd check that the horn is working.

A: Turn on ignition if necessary. Check is carried out by pressing the horn (or center of steering wheel) and listening for the sound.

Q: Tell me where you'd find the info for the recommended tyre pressures for this car.

A: You can find this in the manufacturer's guide, or sometimes on a sticker inside the driver's door shut, or inside the fuel filler flap.

Q: Show me how you'd clean the windscreen using the washers and wipers.

A: Turn on ignition if necessary. Operate the right-hand stalk (usually by pulling it towards you) to spray washer fluid and activate wipers.
